theOXREVIEW

WELCOME

'word-of-mouth'-like content stored online for easier access

theOXREVIEW is a platform created for the student population of Oxford which digitalises the concept of word-of-mouth. In a similar way that you'd ask your college parents, use this to find out what optional modules would be best suited for you, and to find out which lecture courses have the best/worst lecturers. Find out where you could go within college for help with an academic or pastoral problem, and ask whatever question you want. theOXREVIEW aims to be a platform on which students can informally review tutors, lecture courses, and give helpful advice to others in their year/in the years below, something that has been done by word of mouth before! In order to be a helpful community, we need users to also give advice/reviews as well, and encourage you to write about your experiences for the student community. All reviews are submitted anonymously, but information about your Year of Study, Subject, and College, will be published alongside each review you make. We've asked you to sign up with your Oxford email address. As with any platform where users are able to post whatever they'd like, this needs to be moderated to an extent. Signing up with an @ox.ac.uk address ensures that only current students can actually leave reviews.
