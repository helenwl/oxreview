help us create a digital library of information for current and future students

theOXREVIEW is made by students, and has been created for the student population of Oxford. In a similar way that you'd ask your college parents, use this tool to find out what optional modules would be best suited for you, or to find out which lecture courses have the best/worst lecturers.... and much more!

All reviews and questions are submitted anonymously, except information about your Year of Study, Subject, and College, will be published alongside each review you make. As with any platform where users are able to post whatever they'd like, this needs to be moderated, and inappropriate comments will be taken down.
